how to
  1. write down your tasks for the day
  2. estimate how many pomos each task will take
  3. set your work and break timers
  4. begin a pomo
  5. take a short break after 1 pomo
  6. take a longer break after completing 1 cycle (4 pomos)
  7. assess your tasks completed and pomo estimates
  8. start a new cycle if needed

*This timer was made for Chrome. Local download availabe for Windows devices only. Look forward to updates that will include Safari and Mac!
